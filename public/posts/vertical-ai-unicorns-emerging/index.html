These aren’t general-purpose AI tools. They’re industry-specific agents that understand domain workflows, regulations, and edge cases.

Why Vertical Wins#

  1. Moats are deeper - Domain expertise + proprietary data
  2. Revenue is clearer - Displacing specific roles/workflows
  3. Competition is thinner - Horizontal players can’t specialize fast enough

The Playbook#

If you’re building in AI:

  • Don’t compete with foundation models
  • Pick a specific industry vertical
  • Build agents, not assistants
  • Own the workflow end-to-end

The next wave of AI unicorns won’t be horizontal platforms. They’ll be vertical agents that quietly run entire industries.
